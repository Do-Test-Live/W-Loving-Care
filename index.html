OUR SERVICES

At Evercare, our goal is to empower your loved ones to live with dignity and independence. As a professional nursing agency, we provide home care services for families and health professional staffing relief for organisations in Hong Kong. Our platform has over 20,000 caregivers who are equipped to handle everything from daily elderly assistance to post-operative care.

Elderly Care

From daily companionship to overnight nursing care, our caregivers can assist elderlies with personal care tasks (e.g bathing, lifting) and specialised medical care (e.g stoma care, home dialysis).
